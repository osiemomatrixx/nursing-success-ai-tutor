Arterial Blood Gas (ABG) Analysis

Parameter Normal Range Acidosis Alkalosis
pH 7.35 – 7.45 < 7.35 > 7.45
PaCO₂ (Respiratory) 35 – 45 mmHg > 45 mmHg < 35 mmHg

Tic-Tac-Toe ABG Method

Create a grid with Acid, Normal, and Base columns. Place pH, PaCO₂, and HCO₃⁻ according to their status. The column with two parameters determines the diagnosis (e.g. pH and PaCO₂ in Acid = Respiratory Acidosis). If the third parameter is opposite, partial compensation is occurring.
